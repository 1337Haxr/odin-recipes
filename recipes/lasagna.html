LASAGNA

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni
  • Ground Beef
  • Spicy italian sausage
  • Basil
  • Tomato whole peeled
  • Parmesean
  • An oven I guess
  • Large Spanish Onion
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  1. Brown the beef on medium high
  2. Put beef in large bowl for now
  3. Repeat 1 and 2 for the sausage
  4. Cook onions on medium low until transluscent
  5. Into the pot goes garlic and red pepper flakes
  6. Crush the whole peeled tomatoes in the pot with the onions
  7. Add basil and the thick hard part of the parmesean block at the top
  8. Bring pot to a boil and then turn the heat down and let simmer for 45 mins
  9. Cook pasta in salted boiling water for 5-8mins or as box instructs
  10. Now add all of this into a baking pan and grate parmesean on top
  11. Place in 350 degree oven covered for 45mins uncovered for 10mins or until cheese is brown and bubbling
  12. Wait 5mins then serve
